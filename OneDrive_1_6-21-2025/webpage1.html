Reduced Inequalities

SDG 10 aims to reduce inequality within and among countries.

View All SDGs

Why It Matters

Inequality threatens long-term social and economic development. It harms poverty reduction and destroys people’s self-worth. Ensuring equal opportunities and reducing disparities of outcome is essential to sustainable development.

Empowerment of all people—regardless of age, sex, disability, race, ethnicity, origin, religion, or economic status—is a key focus.
